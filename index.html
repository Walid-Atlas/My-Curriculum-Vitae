  • Documentary Film : Urbi e Ghorba

    Film Maker and Co-Author / 2020-2021 (Pre-Production / Production /Post-Production)

    • Winner of the Turin 2021 immigration festival
    • Ghorba is Arabic and can probably be best translated as "foreignness" or "otherness". It is, however, more a feeling, a self-attribution, a concept that contains all the facets that come with being a "stranger". Four Moroccans that ended up in Turin in different ways, discuss what "Ghorba" means for them, why and how they decided to leave Morocco, they talk about the obstacles that they were confronted with during their journey and about how they experience daily racism, bureaucracy and exclusion. The 30 minute long documentary is an attempt to liberate oneself from this isolation, to tell one‘s own story and to show a non-white perspective on European migration politics.
